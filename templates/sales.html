What is this page?

Sales is the record of everything your store has sold. Every sale here triggers a vendor payout calculation and updates your revenue reports.

Recording a Sale

  • Click + Add Sale right when an item sells
  • Select the item from inventory — it auto-fills the vendor and price
  • Enter the actual sale price (may differ from asking price if discounted)
  • Select payment method: cash, card, etc.
  • Submit — the item status changes to Sold automatically

What Happens After a Sale

  • The vendor's unsettled balance increases by their commission %
  • Your store revenue increases by your cut
  • The item disappears from Active inventory
  • It shows on the vendor's portal so they can see it sold
Never batch sales at end of day — record each one as it happens. It keeps vendors happy and your books accurate.
