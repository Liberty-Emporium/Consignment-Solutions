What is this page?

Items is your complete inventory — everything currently in the store, who it belongs to, what it's priced at, and where it's located.

Adding an Item

  • Click + Add Item (or Vendor Add Item if the vendor is logging their own)
  • Fill in: name, description, asking price, vendor, shelf location, category
  • Add a photo — items with photos sell faster
  • Set status to Active to make it visible and for-sale

Item Statuses

  • Active — on the floor, available for sale
  • Sold — sold, pending vendor settlement
  • Returned — given back to vendor, no longer in store
  • Donated — not sold, donated to charity
Reduce item prices after 30 days unsold — slow-moving inventory ties up your shelves. Use the AI Assistant to help price items competitively.
