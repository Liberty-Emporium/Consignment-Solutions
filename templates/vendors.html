What is this page?

Vendors are the people or businesses whose items you sell on consignment. Every item in your store belongs to a vendor, and every sale owes a vendor their cut.

Adding a Vendor

  • Click + Add Vendor and fill in: name, email, phone, commission rate
  • Set their commission split — e.g., 60% to vendor, 40% to store
  • Each vendor gets login access to check their own items and balance

Vendor Portal

  • Vendors can log in at /vendor/login to see their items, sales, and pending settlements
  • This eliminates the constant "what sold?" calls from vendors
  • Send new vendors their login credentials right when you add them
Set commission rates clearly when onboarding a vendor — disputes about splits are the #1 source of vendor friction in consignment stores.
