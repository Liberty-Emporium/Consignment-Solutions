What is this page?

Shelves are the physical storage/display locations in your store. Every item is assigned to a shelf so you and your staff can find anything instantly.

Setting Up Shelves

  • Create one shelf per physical area: "Front Wall A", "Rack 3", "Glass Case", "Back Room B"
  • You can set a maximum capacity per shelf to track how full each area is
  • Shelves can be assigned to specific vendors (rented shelves) or shared

Why This Matters

  • When a customer asks "where is the blue lamp?", you check the item record and it tells you the shelf
  • Shelf assignments help staff restock and tidy specific areas without confusion
  • Rented shelves charge vendors a monthly fee — track it here
Label your physical shelves with the same names you use in the app. Consistency prevents "we can't find the item" problems.
