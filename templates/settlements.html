What is this page?

Settlements is how you pay your vendors. It calculates exactly what each vendor is owed based on their sales since the last payment, then records when you've paid them.

Running a Settlement

  • Select a vendor and click Generate Settlement
  • Review the itemized list — every sale included with amounts
  • Deduct any shelf rent or fees if applicable
  • Click Mark Paid when you've handed over the money or sent a transfer

Settlement Schedule

  • Most consignment stores settle monthly or bi-weekly — be consistent
  • Set a clear pay day with each vendor when they sign up
  • The Unsettled Balance on the dashboard tells you total outstanding at all times
Email vendors their settlement statement when you pay — it builds trust and eliminates disputes. The app generates a printable summary you can attach.
